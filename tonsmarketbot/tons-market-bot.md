# Tons Market Bot

You can visit the bot here : [TONS Market](https://t.me/tonsmarket\_bot)

### What can TonsBot do ?

* List ton-20 tokens
* Inscribe ton-20 tokens
  * Mint ton-20
  * Deploy ton-20
  * Transfer ton-20
* Market ton-20
  * Place buy order
  * Take buy order
  * Place sell order
  * Take sell order
